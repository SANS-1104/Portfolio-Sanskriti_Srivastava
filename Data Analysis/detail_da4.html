Project Description

I demonstrated my data analytics and visualization expertise by analyzing Zomato sales data using Python (Pandas) and Power BI. The project involved cleaning, transforming, and visualizing sales trends, customer demographics, and geographical performance. Key insights included top-performing cities, customer retention trends, and yearly sales fluctuations. By uncovering customer behavior, product demand, and sales hotspots, I provided actionable insights to optimize Zomato's business strategy. This project highlights my ability to handle large datasets, create interactive dashboards, and drive data-driven decision-making.

Key Insights

1. Quantitative Metrics

  - Total Sales Amount: 987M

  - Total Quantity Sold: 2M

  - Total Orders: 150K

2. Top Cities for Sales

  - Leading Cities: Tirupati, Electronic City

  - High Demand Areas: Raipur, Malviya Nagar, Bikaner

3. Yearly Sales Trends

  - Peak Sales: 2018 (0.42B)

  - Decline in Sales: 2020 (0.14B)

4. Customer Analysis

  - Lost Customers: 33K (more females than males)

  - New Customers: 12K (mostly males)

  - Primary User Age Group: 20-30 years

5. Customer Retention Focus

  - High customer loss suggests a need for retention strategies, especially for female users.
